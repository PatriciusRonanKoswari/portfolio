Let's Flashback For a Moment

Our High School Stories

Our meeting was at the beginning of 10th grade. Let me retell the story of how we first met. Every time we start a new level in school, there is always something called "MPLS" or an introduction to the school environment. This activity was held to get to know the school environment and get to know other new students. It was during this time that I saw you for the first time, and I liked you. I watched the way you sat, your smile, until I tried to get your attention. Slowly by slowly I began to find out what your name is and began to approach you. Until the time comes and I'm ready to confess my love to you. On August 1, 2017 you officially became my girlfriend.

"2017, August 1"

Our Relationship Struggles

If we remember, we experienced quite a lot of relationship struggles during our school days. But in relationships, disagreements are not uncommon. Precisely it can strengthen and bring us closer again. In the past, we still had a hard time dating because we couldn't drive a vehicle. So, our date is 80% in the school area. Thanks to you, I became a studious person and started thinking about my future. Before I met you, I was a lazy person and my only job was to hang out with friends almost every day. Spending money without thinking about the future. Since I was with you, I began to realize that I want to live with you forever. Therefore, I want to change to be a better person in order to be able to live with you in the future.

Is There Any Progress in Our Relationship?

Yes of course. Each of our problems can be passed over time. Thanks to God for giving us the opportunity to live this relationship. Now we can date wherever we want. We can spend more quality time together than before. The important thing is that we are getting closer to each othe and getting to know each other's families. I know problems in relationships will always arise, but I promise to keep this relationship because I love you. Problems are not a reason to end a relationship.

About Our Date

In this segment, I'll tell you about the things I love about dating you. I like to pick you up from your house, because I can see your pretty smile. I like that we go together to our date place. I like us holding hands when we go out with you. I like to eat the food I like while looking at you in front of you. I love the ambiguity we both have when it comes to jokes. I like watching movies on dates with you because I can hold hands and lean on you. After watching, I like it when we buy dessert or other snacks. The last one is that I like that we spend time communicating until evening until I have to go home. What I don't like is when I have to part with you because we will meet again next week.

My Message For You Alen

Thank you for accepting me as your partner. I am so grateful to have lived these 5 years with you. Chat every day, Dating every week. I once imagined that I had never met you, wasting money and didn't know the meaning of saving, was lazy to study, and had no meaning in life. With your presence, I found my purpose in life. I want to try so that later in the future I can live with you forever. Thank you for being a patient, caring and loving woman. I'm here to try to be more patient and be able to make you my queen.

Promise to Love and Take Care of You

~Ronan~
